About Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi, the current Prime Minister of India, is known for his strong leadership and implementation of various economic and social reforms. He rose from a humble background and served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat before assuming the Prime Ministerial role in 2014. His policies emphasize development, digitalization, and international relations

Accomplishments

  • Implementation of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) in India to streamline the indirect taxation system
  • Launching various initiatives such as Make in India, Digital India, and Swachh Bharat Abhiyan to boost manufacturing, digital infrastructure, and sanitation across the country.
  • Promotion of renewable energy sources and the International Solar Alliance to combat climate change and promote sustainable development.

Legacy

Narendra Modi's legacy encompasses economic reforms, digital advancements, proactive diplomacy, social initiatives, and a vision for a prosperous and united India, fostering national pride and inclusive growth.
